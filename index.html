Michael Mlikotic "Kotek" Is an experimental electronic artist who strives to break the boundaries between multiple genres. Taking influence from Edm and combining it with an organic undertone that stems from his roots in metal and Jazz, Kotek has created a signature sound best described as "Industrial, Glitchrock."
